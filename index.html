SEDRA PROJECT Charts

SEDRA (Selective Entry Dynamic Risk Assessment) is a mathematical model aimed to calculating a global Safety Index for Covid-19. By calculating the risk that an "undetected carrier" passenger may arrive from any given Country, this model can assess how safe it is to keep the borders open with that Country, and can help regulators take appropriate precautions in order to reduce the risk of importing viruses from abroad. For more info please refer to the project description.
